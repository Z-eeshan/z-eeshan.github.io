The Journey!

My career journey began unexpectedly when the pandemic delayed my graduation, allowing me to start as a System Administrator before completing my degree. After graduating, I worked as a Lab Instructor at my university, where I earned the Google IT Support Certification. This experience gave me a strong foundation in both IT support and teaching.

I later transitioned into a DevOps role, where I focus on CI/CD pipeline configuration with GitLab, task automation using Ansible and shell scripting, and containerizing apps with Docker for Kubernetes deployments. I also worked with a US-based organization to set up their AWS CI/CD pipeline using CodeCommit, CodeBuild, CodeDeploy, and CodePipeline.

I’m now a Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) and preparing for the AWS Certified SysOps Administrator certification. Outside of work, I enjoy baking, walking, and exploring nature—activities that help me stay creatively inspired and recharge.
